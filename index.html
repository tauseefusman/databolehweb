How It Works

Our Approach to Turning Your Data into Insights

1

Understand Your Business

We go through discovery meetings with you to understand your business goals and obstacles. You may have asked questions like:

  • Are there days in the week where my business is slower?
  • Which products sales are dropping?
  • Should I run promotions on these products?
  • What will my margins be if I offer promotions?
  • Are there seasons where some items sell out fast and I should stock up for?

We gather all these questions during the discovery meetings.

2

Gather and Engineer Your Data

Your business generates data in many ways:

  • Transactional systems (cash registers, e-commerce, CRM)
  • Customer in-app/online interactions
  • Business supplies and inventory systems
  • Marketing and social media platforms

Our data engineering team designs the right infrastructure using:

  • AWS cloud services (S3, Redshift, Glue, Lambda)
  • Azure solutions (Data Factory, Synapse Analytics)
  • Snowflake data warehousing for seamless scaling
  • Databricks for unified data processing

Once we assess your data landscape, we build scalable pipelines that process your data reliably and efficiently.

3

Apply Data Science & AI

Our data scientists apply advanced analytics to discover patterns in your data:

  • Statistical analysis to identify trends and correlations
  • Machine learning models for predictive insights
  • AI agents to automate decision processes
  • NLP to extract insights from text data

We focus on creating actionable models that directly address your business questions and provide clear recommendations.

4

Visualize with BI Tools

We deliver insights through interactive dashboards using leading BI platforms:

  • Power BI for comprehensive dashboards with drill-down capabilities
  • Tableau for advanced visualizations and storytelling
  • ThoughtSpot for AI-powered analytics and natural language queries
  • Custom solutions for specialized needs

Your dashboards are designed to be intuitive, accessible on any device, and provide clear answers to your business questions in real-time.
